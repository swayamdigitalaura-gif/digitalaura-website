What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is a term used to label all the marketing practices that utilize smartphones, laptops, tablets, and any other device connected to the internet. The platforms for using digital marketing are numerous including, apps, websites, blogs, SEO, online content, email and social media. Digital marketing efforts are one of the most beneficial ways to use for communication with potential consumers. Meanwhile, the traditional marketing techniques can only feature on limited platforms. The mediums used in traditional marketing do not allow as direct communication as the innovative digital marketing efforts.

What is the difference between digital marketing and digital advertising?

Digital marketing is the use of strategies and techniques that aids businesses to raise brand awareness, share product information, improve audience engagement, monitor their online presence and overall reputation. Meanwhile, digital advertising allows businesses to promote, advertise and display various products, offer special discounts and reduced prices, and other practices used to garner the buying customer’s attention. Digital marketing is used to build a target customer audience, while digital advertising aids in taking these potential customers and converting them into profitable sales, establishing a loyal customer base for the brand.

How long does it take before I see results?

It is common for businesses to expect sudden and effective results on the implementation of practices. The paid forms of digital marketing such as Pay Per Click and paid social media campaigns provide quicker results. But when businesses tend to use organic techniques like content marketing, email list building, and search engine optimization, business owners need to understand the importance of continuous practice and patience. The significant results of all the hard work might be noticeable in a matter of months. However, it takes at least six months to a year to register the improvements in your traffic and other conversion objectives.
