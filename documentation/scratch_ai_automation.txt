What Advanced Automation Actually Looks Like in Production
Case Study 01
Residential Property Investment Platform  |  Sydney, Australia
THE PROBLEM
A PropTech firm had a team of two analysts manually monitoring 11 property listing portals daily, filtering by 23 investment criteria, and emailing shortlisted properties to a WhatsApp investor group. Average response time from listing live to investor notification: 6–8 hours. They were consistently losing deals to faster-moving buyers.
WHAT WE BUILT
A real-time AI monitoring pipeline that continuously watches 11 portals via structured data feeds. Every new listing passes through a GPT-4 vision-powered classification layer that scores it against the firm's 23 investment criteria, cross-references against historical sale data, and generates a formatted investment brief with risk flag, yield estimate, and suburb trend analysis. Qualifying listings trigger an instant WhatsApp broadcast with the AI-generated brief attached — within 90 seconds of the listing going live.
TECH STACK
Custom Python pipeline  ·  GPT-4 Vision  ·  WhatsApp Business API  ·  PostgreSQL  ·  AWS Lambda
RESULTS
Notification time reduced from 6–8 hours to under 90 seconds
2 full-time analyst roles reallocated to high-value deal structuring
Investor response rate on shortlisted properties increased 67%
4 additional acquisitions in the first quarter attributed to speed advantage
"We had analysts doing what this system does, but slower and only during business hours. This thing works at 2am on a Sunday and it doesn't miss a listing."
— Head of Acquisitions, Sydney PropTech Firm
Case Study 02
Precision Manufacturing Group  |  Birmingham, UK
THE PROBLEM
A mid-sized manufacturer was catching product defects at end-of-line QC, after hours of production had already run. Defect rates were averaging 3.8% — resulting in significant material waste, client complaints, and rework costs exceeding £180,000 annually.
WHAT WE BUILT
A real-time computer vision quality control system integrated directly into the production line. Industrial cameras capture images at 12 frames per second. A custom-trained vision model — fine-tuned on 40,000+ labelled images of their specific product defects — analyses each frame in real time. Defect detected → line paused → image annotated with defect classification and location → production supervisor alerted on mobile with full context → root cause analysis automatically surfaced from historical defect patterns.
TECH STACK
Custom CNN model (PyTorch)  ·  Edge inference hardware  ·  Real-time alert API  ·  Defect analytics dashboard  ·  MQTT event bus
RESULTS
Defect detection rate improved from 3.8% to 0.3%
Annual rework costs reduced by £162,000 in Year 1
Detection moved from end-of-line to within 80ms of defect occurrence
Root cause data surfaced patterns that led to upstream process changes
"We've been doing manual QC for 22 years. What Digital Aura built in 6 weeks has fundamentally changed how we think about production intelligence."
— Operations Director, Birmingham Manufacturing Group
Case Study 03
Commercial Law Firm  |  Dublin, Ireland
THE PROBLEM
Senior associates at a 40-partner commercial law firm were spending 5–7 hours per contract reviewing new agreements — extracting key dates, obligations, liability clauses, and non-standard terms, then producing an internal risk summary memo before partner review. A firm bottleneck that was limiting throughput and billing capacity.
WHAT WE BUILT
An intelligent contract review pipeline custom-trained on 8 years of the firm's own contract library and internal risk taxonomy. New contract uploaded via a secure web portal → NLP pipeline extracts 40+ defined clause types → each clause scored against the firm's risk matrix → non-standard terms flagged and compared against precedent → a structured 2-page risk summary memo auto-generated in the firm's own formatting → delivered to the assigned partner in under 6 minutes.
TECH STACK
Custom fine-tuned LLM  ·  Secure document ingestion API  ·  Clause extraction pipeline  ·  RAG over firm's contract precedent library  ·  Auto-generated DOCX output
RESULTS
Initial review time reduced from 5–7 hours to under 6 minutes
340+ contracts processed in the first 5 months
Associates freed to focus on negotiation, advice, and client-facing work
Zero material clauses missed across all processed documents
"We were skeptical that AI could handle the nuance our work requires. The system doesn't just extract text — it understands context within our specific legal framework. It's become indispensable."

Case Study 04
Mortgage Brokerage Network  |  Ontario, Canada
THE PROBLEM
A brokerage network processing 200+ applications per month had a 3-person team manually reviewing applications, verifying document completeness, cross-referencing lender criteria across 18 products, and producing recommendation memos. Average processing time per application: 4 hours. The bottleneck was creating a 6-day average queue for applicants.
WHAT WE BUILT
An end-to-end AI application intelligence system. Edge cases and complex files routed to human review with AI pre-analysis already attached.
TECH STACK
Multimodal document processing  ·  Custom lender scoring engine  ·  LLM memo generation  ·  React Native broker app  ·  Supabase real-time backend
RESULTS
Processing time reduced from 4 hours to 22 minutes average
Application queue reduced from 6 days to same-day processing
Broker capacity increased 3.4x without additional hires
Lender match accuracy rated higher than manual process by brokers
"Our brokers now spend their time advising clients, not reading bank statements. The quality of recommendations has actually gone up because the AI doesn't get tired at the end of the day."

HEADLINE:
Common Questions
Q: How is this different from what we can build ourselves using Make or Zapier?
Platforms like Make and Zapier are excellent for connecting apps using pre-built triggers and actions. What we build is different — custom AI layers that reason, classify, generate content, and make conditional decisions based on your data. When your workflow involves language, documents, images, or complex business logic, no-code tools hit their ceiling quickly.
Q: Do we need to replace our existing systems?
Almost never. We engineer integrations into your existing CRM, ERP, databases, and tools. The AI automation layer sits on top of what you already have and makes it smarter.
Q: How do you handle sensitive data — legal documents, financial records, patient data?
We architect with data privacy as a non-negotiable. Sensitive workloads are processed on private infrastructure, never through shared cloud AI APIs. We are GDPR-aligned and can work within your existing compliance framework.
Q: What does the AI do when it encounters something it's not confident about?
Every system we build includes a human-in-the-loop escalation path. Low-confidence events are flagged to the right person with full context — the AI tells you what it found, why it's uncertain, and what it recommends. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Q: How long does it take to build?
PoC in 2 weeks. Production deployment in 4–6 weeks for most systems. Measurable ROI typically visible within the first 30 days of live operation.
Q: How do we get started?
